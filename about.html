About Us

Potomato Art and Food (opc) pvt.ltd

is company established in 2018. we provide product series Eat-Play Yoga to educate kids with their own taste.Along with colorful chocolates,our product consists of toys, yoga flash cards, finger puppets and ballons. It is nothing but A new way of enjoyment for kids which could make parents more comfortable.We take a look at the importance of building skills from a young age, and what you can do to support your child.

Vision

To nurture kids in all directions Engage a child’s senses, spark their imaginations and encourage them to interact with others

Mission

  • Whatever a child ends up doing as a job, they have to be able to work with other people.
  • They also need to build relationships, whether with family members, friends &neighbors.
  • To form a product series to educate kid’s with their own taste.
  • To create new way of enjoyment for kids which could make parents more comfortable.
  • To provide best quality product services and customer support in order to form healthy minds.
